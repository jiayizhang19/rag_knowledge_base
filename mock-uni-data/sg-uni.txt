The National University of Singapore (NUS) offers a Master of Computing with several specializations including Artificial Intelligence and Data Science. Admission Requirements: Applicants need a minimum GPA of 3.5/4.0 or equivalent. International students must provide an IELTS score of at least 7.0. Tuition Fees: Approximately 52,000 SGD for international students. Application Deadline: January 15th for the August intake.
